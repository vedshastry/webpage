About

I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC).
My research studies the effects that climate has on agriculture and structural change in developing economies. I was previously a consultant with the World Bank's Development Impact Group (DECDI/DIME) and a research assistant at the Indian School of Business (ISB).
