About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC). My research studies the effects that climate has on agriculture and structural change in developing economies.

Previously, I was a consultant with the World Bank's Development Impact Group (DECDI/DIME) and a research assistant at the Indian School of Business (ISB).

Beyond academia, I enjoy singing, hiking and playing chess. :)
