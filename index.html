Research Focus

I pursue research in development economics, focusing on agriculture, climate, and poverty. My current work involves assessing the effectiveness of school-based interventions in improving educational outcomes and food security.

Current Project Highlight

World Food Programme School-Based Interventions in Burundi

As part of the World Bank's Development Impact Evaluation (DIME) team, I'm currently working on evaluating the impact of the World Food Programme's school-based meal interventions in Burundi. This project aims to understand how these interventions affect educational outcomes, nutrition, and overall welfare of school children.
