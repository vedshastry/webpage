About

I work as a short-term consultant/research assistant with the World Bank at Development Impact Evaluation (DIME). I pursue research in development economics and policy. My goal is to examine how agriculture is impacted by climate shifts, and in understanding scalable interventions to build livelihood networks.

I enjoy learning, no matter what the topic is! I am an avid enthusiast of sports, music, travel, and languages.
