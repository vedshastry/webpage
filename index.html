About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC). My research studies how climate, agriculture and infrastructure impact structural transformation in developing countries.

I have previously assisted projects on similar themes with the World Bank's Development Impact Group (DIME/DECDI) and at the Indian School of Business (ISB).

Beyond academia, I am enthusiastic about computers and technology. I maintain a few repos from my workflow on Github. I enjoy playing chess, table tennis tournaments, the guitar, singing and occasionally songwriting.
