About

I am a short term consultant/research assistant with DIME, the Development Impact group at World Bank.

I pursue research in development economics. My goal is to understand how agricultural livelihoods are affected by climate, and designing scalable interventions using the tools of economics.

I am also interested in astronomy, chess and table tennis.
