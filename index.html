About

I am a consultant with the World Bank at Development Impact Evaluation (DIME), working on research in development economics. My goal is to examine how agriculture is impacted by climate shifts, and in understanding scalable interventions to build livelihood networks.

I enjoy learning, no matter what the topic is! I am an avid enthusiast of sports, music, travel, and languages.
